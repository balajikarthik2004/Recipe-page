Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick, perfect for any meal. This classic Omelette combines beasten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetable, or meats

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximate 10 minutes
  • Prepartion:5 minutes
  • cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large egges
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • opyional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats,herbs

instructions

1.

Best the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. you can add atablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.

2.

Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and butter or oil.

3.

Cook the Omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling. pou in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

4.

Add filling(optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen filings over one half of the Omelette.

5.

Fold and serve: As the Omelette contines to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. let it cook for another minute, then sidle it onto a plate

6.

Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if nedded.
